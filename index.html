1 - The extension flow for Blueground’s mobile app

Personal contribution: UX Research and Product Design

THE PROBLEM

People are not able to extend their stay through the app at the moment. In order to extend their stay, the need to connect with a call center and define availability and price through a series of emails

THE SOLUTION

Allow the user to extend their stay through the app, taking in considerations the cases in which their current apartment is available and providing them with an estimated price for their extension

Step 1 - Competitor Analysis

We compared the current extension flows of our competitors focusing on what works and what doesn’t

Step 2 - Competitor user flows

Studies the competitor’s user flows and made sure we identified common grounds and solutions. We explored how those could be incorporated

Step 3 - Prioritazation matrix

We studied the benefits and challenges that we would have by adding this feature in the current stage of the app

Step 4 - Location

Based on the user testing and IA workshop we identified potential locations for this feature

Step 5 - Competitor user flows

We studies the user flows in all the potential scenarios based on availability

Step 6 - Prioritazation matrix

We compared the different calendar visualization of the competitors

Step 7 - Mid Fidelity Prototype

We created a mid fidelity prototype including the company’s current style guide

FINALE

As per March 2021, the extension option was not available through the web or the mobile app of Blueground. The team is working on developing the booking option through the app. Once that is complete they will be moving on to the extension
